who we are

Our Story

Born out of a shared love for the warm, crackling embrace of vinyl, Dublin Vinyl Club was founded by a group of dedicated music aficionados. Our journey began with a simple idea—to create a space where vinyl enthusiasts could come together to explore, share, and revel in the beauty of analog music.

The Vinyl Experience

Step into Dublin Vinyl Club, and you'll be immersed in a curated world of sonic richness. We believe in the unmatched charm of vinyl records, where each groove tells a story and every needle drop is a moment suspended in time. Our collection spans genres and eras, ensuring there's something for everyone—from the classics that defined an era to the hidden gems waiting to be discovered.

Our Commitment

Dublin Vinyl Club is more than a subscription service; it's a community built on a shared love for the unparalleled experience of vinyl. We are committed to preserving the essence of analog sound, fostering a sense of belonging among our members, and contributing to the enduring legacy of vinyl in the digital age.

Join the Club

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just beginning your vinyl journey, Dublin Vinyl Club welcomes you to join our community. Let's embark on a sonic adventure together—a celebration of music, nostalgia, and the enduring magic of vinyl.
